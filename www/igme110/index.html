Matthew Discavage: A Short Bio

Hello, everyone! My name is Matthew Discavage, and I am a current first-year student at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I graduated last year from Gates Chili High School, and I am majoring in Game Design & Development. While this major focuses on coding and software development, I also have a musical background. I have been singing and playing clarinet since 4th grade, and I have also been composing musical works occasionally since summer 2016. As a matter of fact, my ideal career is to become a video game composer, combining both my passions for games and music, but a non-musical job as a game developer would be just as rewarding. At the current moment, I have not found an opportunity to organize my compositions together into a portfolio, but a youtube video of the first piece I ever composed can be found on a friend's channel here (the nickname I was credited with is my old Youtube username for the sake of anonymity; it has since been changed).

As mentioned, games and music are my two main interests. When it comes to games, I'm mainly a Nintendo fan, with Super Mario Bros. and Pokemon being my favorite franchises. I have lately been playing Super Mario Maker 2 quite a bit, and I have spent weeks designing a series of traditional levels that wouldn't look out of place in an actual Mario game (they are sorted into 8 worlds of 8 levels, and the entirety of World 1 is currently uploaded). On the music side of things, I am currently a part of one of RIT's acapella groups, Surround Sound. I also like playing Dungeons & Dragons with my friends occasionally, though I have not found the time to do so since joining college. If anyone reading this is a fan of D&D, please let me know!
